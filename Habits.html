HABITS

  1. Practice Voice Exercises
    Why? Because I want to speak openly and confidently with my natural voice.

  2. Body and Posture Awareness
    Why? Because it helps me relax my body's tension and so I can learn to what my body is telling me.

  3. Breath Awareness and Deep Breathing
    Why? Because it brings me back in the present moment and helps to keep the emotions in balance.

  4. Develop Concentration
    Why? Because we live in a world of distractions and concentration is one of the most valuable skills to develop to succeed in life.

  5. Emotional State Awareness
    Why? Because emotions are the driving force behind all of my actions and becoming aware of emotions is the first steo of mastering them.
